04 — Buttons

Button System

Two families, four variants. Family 1 (filled pill, inner blue glow) sits over dark backgrounds and event cards. Family 2 (white outlined pill, outer blue glow) is reserved for the standalone CTA strip at the bottom of dark sections.

Type 1A — Primary CTA Hero, attendee action. Grey fill, inner blue glow. Helvetica Bold 20.
Type 1B — Card Primary Inside event cards. Same style as 1A, slightly larger (24px type, 19.2/28.8 padding).
Type 1C — Sponsor CTA Hero, sponsor action. Cyan fill, cyan border, inner blue glow.
Type 2 — White Outer Glow Bottom strip CTA. White fill, outer blue glow, 80px radius.

When to use which

ContextButton
Hero — attendee action1A · standard primary
Event cards — register/ticket1B · card-size primary
Hero — sponsor action1C · cyan sponsor

08 — Voice & Tone

How Hivemind Sounds

Confident, direct, industry-insider. Speaks to senior gaming professionals as peers. Authoritative but not arrogant. Specific over vague. Energy without hype. Community-first framing.

Tone by context

ContextToneExample
Event headlinesBold, declarative“Powering the Future of B2B Gaming Events”
Event descriptionsWarm, inviting“Each activity is designed to turn conversations into collaborations.”
CTA copyDirect, action-first“Get Tickets” / “Become a Sponsor”
Social / testimonialAuthentic, peer-to-peer“Simply one of the best side-events of the year.”
Stats / proof pointsConfident, factual“70% Executives & Founders / 1500+ Attendees / 12+ Events”

Use these patterns

  • Use “Curated” — implies craft, not gatekeeping
  • Use “Connecting the B2B games industry”
  • Use “Free and paid mixers” — transparency about access
  • Use “Conversations into collaborations”

Avoid these

  • Avoid “World-class” — generic
  • Avoid “Networking event” — too transactional, prefer mixer
  • Avoid “Amazing opportunity” — filler
  • Avoid “State-of-the-art” — irrelevant

10 — Asset Checklist

Before you ship

Every Hivemind World branded asset must clear this list.

  • Background is #070725 or a photo over #070725
  • All text is #ffffff, #f8fafc, or #e2e8f0
  • Section headings use Scandia Bold
  • Sub-headings, body, buttons use Helvetica
  • At least one atmospheric light streak on full dark sections
  • Attendee CTAs use Type 1A or 1B
  • Sponsor CTAs use Type 1C (cyan)
  • Standalone strip CTAs use Type 2 (white outer glow)
  • #4d7eff blue glow appears on every button
  • Event cards use 16px radius
  • Pill buttons use 96px (Type 1) or 80px (Type 2) radius
  • Logo is white version, never isolated for sub-brands
  • Copy uses Hivemind voice — direct, specific, insider
