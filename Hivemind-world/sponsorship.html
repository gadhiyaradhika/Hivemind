Questions

Frequently asked.

Still not sure? Drop us a note at harry@hivemind.world.

What's included in the media pack?

Founders, publishers, investors, and senior leaders in studios who are actively working on games, growth, or deals. If you're primarily selling into the games industry as a vendor, you're welcome to apply, but we prioritize the people building and greenlighting games.

How many sponsors do you take per event?

The Barcelona Mixer is a paid, ticketed event. A modest cover keeps the room curated and covers food, drinks, and venue. Sponsors and select partners may receive complimentary access.

Can we sponsor multiple events at a discount?

The value of the room comes from who's in it. Applications let us balance the mix of studios, publishers, investors, and partners so every conversation is worth your time.

What data do we get post-event?

Each application is reviewed by the team within 48 hours. If you're confirmed, you'll receive venue details, the attendee list preview, and your ticket link.

Can we speak at the event as a sponsor?

Yes — each colleague should apply individually so we can confirm fit. Teams of 2–3 are common; larger groups should reach out to us directly.

Ready to put your brand in the room?

Applications reviewed within 3 business days. Limited packages per event.
